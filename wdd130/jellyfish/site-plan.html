Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform potential customers about the different packages and options offered by Pirate Oar Rafting. Specifically, this page will educate individuals on:

  1. Available tours
    • Jolly Roger - very small rough areas, might require slight maneuvering.
    • Monkey Island - some rough water, maybe some rocks, might require some maneuvering.
    • Treasure Island - small waves, maybe a small drop, but no considerable danger; may require significant maneuvering.
    • Blackbeard´s Ghost - whitewater, medium waves, maybe rocks, maybe a considerable drop, sharp maneuvers may be needed.
    • Deadman´s Chest - whitewater, large waves, large volume, possibility of large rocks and hazards, possibility of a large drop, requires precise maneuvering.
  2. What makes us different?
    • costumes welcome
    • 150 question survey
    • you are guaranteed to find treasure on every trip
  3. The precautions and recommendations needed to confidently and safely run a river
    • weather
    • physical health
  4. Necessary equipment
    • inflatable raft
    • oars
    • life jacket
    • helmet
    • wetsuit / suimsuit / comfortable clothing
  5. Pricing
    • discounts
    • group deals
  6. Guest responsibilities
  7. Location and contact information

Prospective clients will also be treated to a gallery of past expeditions and testimonials.

Audience

Who are our customers?

Our customers are small to large teams, families, and all those who seek for treasure. Our customers range from age 12 to 75; male and female alike.

What do they want?

Our customers want adventure. When they book a tour with Pirate Oar Rafting they generally show up dressed as a pirate. Think escape room only on the water. Using our patented 150 question survey, we tailor each experience to fit the needs of the individual group.

What are their needs and wants?

Our customers need to know 1. types of tours that are available, 2. what makes our tours different 3. where we operate and how to contact us. They also want information on package pricing, equipment and assurances that their experience with us will be safe.

How will the audience access the site?

Pre-adventure, customers will access our site via their desktop. During the trip however, they will access our site via their mobile devices. Think digital treasure map.

Typography

Heading Font: Charm

This humanistic font is fun yet readable. Using this in the headings is a stylistic choice meant to emphasis our companies commitment to having fun.

Paragraph Font: Montserrat, Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif

Using this font for text heavy parts of the page will make it easier to read and understand the information. It shows we care about our customers and value the time they spend on our site.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, Pirate Oar Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.

Colored paragraph example

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced rafters. No matter what type of river adventures you are seeking, Pirate Oar Rafting Company can make it happen for you.
